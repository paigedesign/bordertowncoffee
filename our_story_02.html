Our Story!

Bordertown was a faternity house that has now turned into a coffee shop. Over a year, volunteers have helped us build into the shop we are day. The Bordertown name comes from a desire to reach beyond ourselves and describes a place where people from different backgrounds live and work together as friends.

The vision for Bordertown started when one of our founders (in his college days) heard about starving children and realized he need to live for more than his own aspirations and entertainment. The photographs in our entry are from a home in the border-town Juarez, Mexico that serves children and families
